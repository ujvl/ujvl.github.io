Overview

I study data-driven algorithmic systems embedded in society, broadly.

I'm advised by Dawn Song, and am affiliated with RDI & IC3. I'm physically based in New York at Cornell Tech. My work has previously been supported by the Ethereum Foundation.

Background. I started my PhD in 2022, focusing primarily on analyzing and building secure, privacy-preserving systems. I've been on leave working on a startup since 2023, but will be returning to grad school at some point (possibly some time in 2026/27). Earlier in my career, I designed and built distributed systems for machine learning applications at the AMPLab/RISELab.

Outside work. Avid reader, boxer (cal boxing '18), photog. Warriors basketball fan. Urban policy enthusiast. Sometimes help some friends with an Indian food popup (follow @dononyc).
